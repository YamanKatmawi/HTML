Real Madrid

The official Real Madrid website closed out 2019 with a monthly average of 2,006,000 unique visitors, according to internet audience measurement firm Comscore. Realmadrid.com finished 2019 as the most visited football club website in the world for the fourth consecutive year, according to figures published by Comscore. Over the course of the year, the monthly average unique visitors to the Real Madrid website was 2,006,000, eclipsing Liverpool, which is second in the rankings with 863,000 unique visitors. Barcelona are in the third place with 812,000 unique visitors.
